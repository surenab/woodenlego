Product Availability

All products on this website(as may be varied from time to time) are available almost everywhere in mainland UK, and by special arrangement (usually incurring additional charges) to the Channel Islands, Northern Ireland, Republic of Ireland, Isle of white, Isle of Man and the Scottish Islands. All products are subject to availability. If the product you order is unavailable, we will inform you (the customer) and offer you a suitable alternative product or inform you when the product will be available. In the event that we are unable to supply the product or any substitute product to you at all, we shall notify you as soon as possible and shall reimburse your payment in full. Occasionally manufacturers will make minor updates/changes to their products, and we reserve the right to supply these even if pictures and descriptions may vary on the listing shown.

Returns Policy

Crepundy hope that you will be very happy with your purchase. However, if you do want to return your goods please see below for details of our returns policy.

You can cancel your order/return your goods within fourteen calendar days of delivery. You must notify us in writing if you intend to your return your goods, please send all the details through to info@gardentoystore.co.uk. Returned goods must be unused and cannot have been assembled or part-assembled. All returned products must be in their original packaging and be suitable for resale.

We will not accept responsibility for loss or damage in transit and therefore suggest that you request a proof of postage when sending goods back to us. Please also ensure that these are securely packaged.

We will not cover the cost of returning the goods unless these are faulty. Please see below for our policy on faulty goods.

Returned items will be checked and a refund will be provided back on to the original payment card within 14 calendar days of receiving the returned goods. You will be notified via e-mail when the refund has been processed.
